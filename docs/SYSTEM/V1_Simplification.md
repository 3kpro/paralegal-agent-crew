# HANDOFF — XELORA V1 SIMPLIFICATION
Date: December 2025  
Status: Directive  
Scope: Vision alignment only (NO scope expansion)

## Objective
Simplify Xelora V1 to maximize traction, clarity, and speed to users.

This handoff reflects an explicit strategic decision:
Xelora V1 is a **Viral Predictability Engine**, not a full marketing platform.

## Core Product Focus (LOCKED)
Xelora V1 consists of TWO primary capabilities only:

1. **Discovery**
   - Zero-input viral discovery
   - Show users what is gaining momentum right now
   - Purpose: Get users from “nothing” to “something”

2. **Validate**
   - User provides a topic, idea, or angle
   - Xelora returns a Viral Score and improvement signals
   - Purpose: Tell users if their idea is worth posting BEFORE they post

These two capabilities together fully define Xelora V1.

## Explicitly Deferred Feature
### Promote (Campaign Intelligence)
- Promote is NOT part of Xelora V1 execution
- Promote remains in VISION.md as a **future phase**
- Promote should be framed as:
  - “On the roadmap”
  - “Coming after traction”
- No Promote UX, flows, or commitments in V1 messaging

This is a deliberate simplification, not a removal.

## Messaging Alignment
Landing page, onboarding, and positioning should reinforce:

> **Discovery • Validate**
>  
> “Find what’s trending.  
> Validate what you want to post.  
> Stop guessing.”

Avoid:
- Platform language
- Over-promising automation
- Multi-step campaign framing

## TikTok & Public Demo Alignment
The product must be demoable in under 30 seconds:
- Show Discovery
- Show Validate
- Show Viral Score
- End with confidence statement

## Instructions
- Integrate this simplification into VISION.md
- Do NOT expand scope
- Do NOT introduce new features
- Preserve Promote only as a future roadmap item
- Ensure language reflects focus, not incompleteness

This change is about **focus, not capability**.
