## Production Tips

- **Format:** Vertical 9:16 (TikTok/Reels)
- **Energy:** High but authentic (not salesy)
- **Pointing:** Point to PIP demo when referencing features
- **Lighting:** Natural (near window) or ring light
- **Captions:** Add auto-captions (80% watch on mute)
- **Hook:** First 3 seconds are critical - nail the "corporate scraps" line
- **CTA Pause:** Brief pause after "Link in bio" for emphasis
- **Background:** Clean, minimal (home office or living room)

## Recording Notes

- Record in **3-5 takes** to get natural delivery
- Watch your pacing - don't rush the hook
- Make eye contact with camera (not yourself on screen)
- Smile when saying "Let's win" - authentic enthusiasm
- Test PIP window size/position before final recording
- Keep demo window large enough to see clearly on mobile
