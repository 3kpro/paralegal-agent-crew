## Risk Mitigation

### Common Challenges & Solutions

**Challenge 1**: Low initial visibility
- **Solution**: Daily buyer request proposals (10-15/day), competitive launch pricing, social media promotion

- **Solution**: Clear gig descriptions, detailed requirements questionnaire, explicit revision limits
