# Xelora Campaign Creation Video Script

**Duration:** 8 Seconds
**Format:** Text-only overlays on screenshots/screen recordings
**Theme:** Professional Modern (Coral/Dark)

---

## 🎬 Script Breakdown

| Time | Text Overlay (Centered, Bold, Modern Typography) | Screen/Visual Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **0:00-0:02** | **STOP GUESSING.** | **Trend Discovery:** Show the "Viral Discovery" screen with multiple trends and their 🔥 **Viral Score** badges (e.g., 88, 92). |
| **0:02-0:04** | **PICK YOUR PATH.** | **Path Selection:** Show Card 3 of the wizard with the three interactive cards: "Discover Viral", "Validate Idea", and "Promote Product". |
| **0:04-0:06** | **PREDICT PERFORMANCE.** | **Content Settings:** Close-up of the "Predicted Viral Score" meter (92) and "Psychometric Analysis" tags in the compact settings view. |
| **0:06-0:08** | **XELORA: PREDICT. CREATE. WIN.** | **Results:** The final "Generated Content" screen showing optimized posts for Twitter, LinkedIn, and TikTok with high-score indicators. |

---

## 📸 Screenshot Requirement Checklist

To create this video, capture the following high-resolution screenshots from the application:

1.  **`SCR_01_DISCOVERY`**: `app/(portal)/campaigns/create` (Step 5 - Trend Discovery).
    *   *Requirement:* Ensure at least 3 trends are visible with "High" (Coral) Viral Score badges.
2.  **`SCR_02_PATHS`**: `app/(portal)/campaigns/create` (Step 3 - Direction Selection).
    *   *Requirement:* Mouse hovering over the "Promote Product" card to show the interactive glow.
3.  **`SCR_03_SETTINGS`**: `app/(portal)/campaigns/create` (Step 6 - Content Preferences).
    *   *Requirement:* Show the "Psychometric Viral Analysis" section and the "Predicted Viral Score" gauge.
4.  **`SCR_04_RESULTS`**: `app/(portal)/campaigns/create` (Step 7 - Content Generation Results).
    *   *Requirement:* Show the multi-platform tab switcher (Twitter, LinkedIn icons) and the "Connect & Post" button.

---

## 💡 Production Notes
*   **Animation Style:** Fast, punchy transitions (0.2s cross-fades or slides).
*   **Color Palette:** Use the Xelora Coral (#e5a491) for key text highlights.
*   **Audio (Suggested):** Fast-paced, high-tech ambient beat with a heavy "thump" on each text transition.
