## Usage Notes

### For Veo:
- Veo excels at cinematic, photorealistic scenes
- Include camera movement descriptions (dolly, orbit, push)
- Specify lighting conditions explicitly
- Works well with human subjects

### For Banana:
- Banana excels at stylized, motion graphics
- Great for UI animations and abstract visuals
- Include style references (flat design, 3D, etc.)
- Specify frame rates for smooth animations

### Brand Guidelines:
- **Primary Colors:** Coral (#FF6B6B), Cyan (#00C7F2), Purple (#A17CF9), Amber (#F59E0B)
- **Background:** Dark slate (#0A0F1F to #1a2030)
- **Typography:** Clean sans-serif, uppercase for emphasis
- **Always Include:** Viral DNA™ trademark symbol
- **Logo:** XELORA wordmark or logomark

### Output Specifications:
- Resolution: 1080p minimum, 4K preferred
- Aspect Ratios: 16:9 (YouTube), 9:16 (TikTok/Reels), 1:1 (Feed)
- Frame Rate: 24fps (cinematic) or 30fps (smooth)
- Duration: Keep under 15 seconds for social
