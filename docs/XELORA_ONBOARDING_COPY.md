# XELORA Onboarding Copy - First-Time User Clarity

**Last Updated:** December 16, 2025
**Purpose:** Improve first-time user experience with clear, benefit-focused copy
**Audience:** New XELORA users (creators, influencers, small businesses)

---

## Current Onboarding Flow

1. **WelcomeAnimation** → Cinematic intro (5 screens)
2. **Dashboard** → First logged-in view
3. **OnboardingTour** → Campaign creation tour (optional)
4. **Onboarding Page** → 4-step setup (interests, topics, profile, socials)

---

## Problems with Current Copy

### ❌ WelcomeAnimation Issues
- **Too abstract:** "Before they see it" - what does this mean?
- **Too poetic:** "Trends reveal themselves to those who listen" - unclear value
- **Missing concrete benefits:** Doesn't explain what XELORA actually does
- **No quick skip:** User must wait through 5 screens to start

### ❌ OnboardingTour Issues
- **Wrong branding:** References "CCAI" instead of "XELORA"
- **Assumes knowledge:** "Create your first viral campaign" - what does that mean?
- **No context:** Doesn't explain what viral scores are or why they matter

### ❌ Onboarding Page Issues
- **Minimal value props:** Steps are functional but don't explain WHY
- **Unclear benefits:** Why select interests? What do trending topics do?
- **Missing motivation:** No clear goal or outcome explained

---

## Improved Copy Strategy

### Core Principles
1. **Clarity over poetry:** Tell users exactly what XELORA does
2. **Benefits first:** Explain the "why" before the "how"
3. **Quick wins:** Show value within 60 seconds
4. **Progressive disclosure:** Don't overwhelm, reveal features gradually
5. **Consistent voice:** Friendly, confident, empowering (not robotic)

### Key Messages to Communicate
- **What XELORA is:** AI-powered viral content prediction platform
- **Core benefit:** Know what will go viral BEFORE you post
- **How it works:** Viral Score™ predicts engagement with 87% accuracy
- **Quick win:** Create your first campaign in 2 minutes
- **Long-term value:** Stop guessing, start growing

## Voice & Tone Guidelines

### Voice Characteristics
- **Confident:** "Know what will go viral" (not "Maybe predict...")
- **Empowering:** "You're engineering virality" (not "The tool does it for you")
- **Clear:** "87% accurate" (not "highly accurate")
- **Friendly:** "Let's create!" (not "Please proceed to create")

### What to Avoid
- ❌ Jargon: "Multi-platform content orchestration"
- ❌ Overpromising: "Guaranteed to go viral"
- ❌ Passive voice: "Content can be generated"
- ❌ Corporate speak: "Leverage our platform to optimize"

### What to Use
- ✅ Numbers: "87% accurate, 0-100 score, 2 seconds"
- ✅ Active verbs: "Know, Create, Publish, Grow"
- ✅ Concrete outcomes: "Go viral, Get engagement, Save time"
- ✅ Conversational: "Let's get started!" (not "Initiate workflow")
