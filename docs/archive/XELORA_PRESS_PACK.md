## 💡 Key Innovation: Viral DNA™

XELORA's core differentiator is its ability to decode the "DNA" of viral content.

### 1. Viral Score™ (Patent Pending)
- **What it is**: A 0-100 score predicting the probability of high engagement.
- **How it works**: Trained on 1M+ data points, analyzing volume, velocity, and sentiment.
- **Accuracy**: 87% validation rate on historical data.

### 2. Viral DNA Visualization (NEW for Jan 2026)
- **Psychometric Analysis**: We don't just say *what* is trending, but *why*.
- **The "DNA" Tags**:
    - **🪝 Hook Type**: (e.g., Contrarian, Secret, Story, News) - The psychological attention grabber.
    - **❤️ Primary Emotion**: (e.g., Fear, Greed, Curiosity, Awe) - The emotional trigger.
    - **💎 Value Prop**: (e.g., Status, Money, Time, Knowledge) - The reward for the reader.
- **User Benefit**: Users can instantly replicate the *psychological structure* of viral hits, not just copy the topic.
