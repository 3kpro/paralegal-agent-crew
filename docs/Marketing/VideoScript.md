## Text Animation Standards

| Element | Entrance | Hold | Exit |
|---------|----------|------|------|
| Questions | Fade in 0.4s | 3s | Fade out 0.3s |
| Step labels | Slide left 0.3s | Duration of clip | Fade out 0.3s |
| Center text | Fade in 0.3s | 4-6s | Fade out 0.3s |
| Logo/URL | Fade in 0.5s | 7s | Fade out 0.5s |
