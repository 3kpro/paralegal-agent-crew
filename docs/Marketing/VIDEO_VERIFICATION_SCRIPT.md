# Google Business Profile - Video Verification Script (Home Office)

**Context:** Google requires video verification to prove you are a legitimate business, even if you are home-based. Since you are a **Service Area Business** (you go to clients or serve them online, they don't come to you), you keep your address hidden from the public, but Google still needs to see it privately to verify you exist.

**Goal:** Provide a continuous, unedited video (usually < 2 mins) proving **Location**, **Equipment**, and **Management**.

---

## 🎬 The Shot List (Do this in one take)

### 1. The Setup (Location)
*   **Start Outside:** Begin the video outside your home.
*   **Street Sign/Number:** Zoom in on your house number or mailbox. This **MUST** match the address you entered in your Google Business Profile backend (even if that address is hidden publicly).
*   **Context:** Pan briefly to show the street/neighborhood (proves it's a real location).

### 2. The Entry (Access)
*   **Walk Up:** Walk to the door.
*   **Unlock the Door:** Show yourself using the key or keypad to unlock the door.
*   **Why:** This proves you have "Manager Access" to the facility and aren't just a visitor.

### 3. The "Commute"
*   **Walk Briskly:** Walk directly to your home office workspace.
*   **Ignore the House:** Do not film the kitchen, bedroom, or family. Focus on getting to the "Productivity Zone."

### 4. The Workspace (Equipment)
*   **The Desk:** Show your multi-monitor setup, laptop, and keyboard.
*   **Software (Method 1 - Best for You):** Have your monitor ON. Show the **Vercel Dashboard** or **Stripe Dashboard** for 3K Pro Services.
    *   *Tip:* Navigate between tabs to show it's live software, not a screenshot.
*   **Code:** Show VS Code open with the `3kpro-website` project visible. This proves you are a software business.

### 5. The Documents (Proof of Business)
*   **Paperwork:** Have an official document on your desk.
    *   LLC Registration
    *   Utility bill with your name/address
    *   Business Card (if you have one)
*   **Focus:** Zoom in on the name/address on the document so it's readable.

---

## 🚫 Common Mistakes to Avoid
*   **Editing:** Do NOT edit the video. It must be one continuous take.
*   **Too Long:** Keep it under 2 minutes. Usually 60-90 seconds is perfect.
*   **Darkness:** Turn on all the lights in the office before you go outside to start.
*   **Voices:** You don't verify by talking. You verify by showing. You can narrate ("Here is my office..."), but visuals matter more.

## 📱 How to Upload
1.  Open the Google Maps app on your phone.
2.  Go to your Business Profile.
3.  Select "Get Verified" -> "Capture Video".
4.  Record it live through the app.
