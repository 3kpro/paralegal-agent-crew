**Last Updated:** December 16, 2025

## What is Helix?

**Helix** is XELORA's built-in AI assistant that helps you navigate the platform, answer questions about your campaigns, and provide strategic marketing advice—all without leaving your current page.

Think of Helix as your personal marketing strategist who:
- Knows what page you're on and provides relevant help
- Can analyze your campaign data and show charts
- Remembers your conversation within a session
- Learns your brand voice and preferences (coming soon)

---

## ✅ What Helix Can Do TODAY (December 2025)

### 1. **Conversational AI Assistance**
**Status:** ✅ LIVE

- Ask questions about XELORA features and get instant answers
- Get strategic advice on content marketing and social media
- Receive context-aware help based on your current page
- Chat naturally using Google Gemini 2.0 Flash

**Example Questions:**
- "How do I create a new campaign?"
- "What's the best time to post on LinkedIn?"
- "Explain viral scores to me"
- "How can I improve my engagement rate?"

### 2. **Context Awareness**
**Status:** ✅ LIVE (Basic)

Helix knows:
- What page you're currently viewing
- Your subscription tier (Free, Pro, Premium)
- Your session history within the current conversation

**What this means:**
- If you're on the Campaigns page and ask "how do I do this?", Helix knows you mean campaigns
- Helix can provide tier-appropriate advice (won't suggest Pro features to Free users)

### 3. **Data Analysis & Charts**
**Status:** ✅ LIVE (via Tool Invocations)

- Query your analytics data using natural language
- Helix can display charts directly in the chat (line, bar, pie, etc.)
- Example: "Show me my campaign performance this week"

### 4. **Brand DNA Integration**
**Status:** ✅ LIVE

- Helix can update your Brand DNA (tone, voice, target audience)
- Example: "Update my brand voice to be more professional"
- Persists across sessions for personalized responses

### 5. **Flexible UI Modes**
**Status:** ✅ LIVE

- **Widget Mode:** Floating chat bubble (bottom-right corner)
- **Expanded Mode:** Full-screen overlay
- **Side Panel Mode:** Docked to the right side of the screen
- **Transparency Mode:** See-through background for multi-tasking
- **Draggable:** Move the widget anywhere on screen (widget mode only)
- **Resizable:** Adjust side panel width (side panel mode only)

### 6. **Free Tier Limitations**
**Status:** ✅ LIVE

- Free users: 7 messages per session
- Pro/Premium users: Unlimited messages
- Clear upgrade prompts when limits are reached

## How to Use Helix Today

### Getting Started
1. Look for the glowing Helix logo (bottom-right corner)
2. Click to open the chat widget
3. Ask a question or type "help" to see what Helix can do

### Pro Tips
- **Be specific:** "How do I improve my Twitter engagement?" is better than "Help with Twitter"
- **Ask follow-ups:** Helix remembers your conversation, so you can build on previous questions
- **Try different modes:** Click the panel icon to switch between widget, expanded, and side panel modes
- **Use transparency mode:** Enable transparency (eye icon) to see your work behind Helix while chatting

### Limitations to Know
- **Free Tier:** 7 messages per session (resets when you close the widget)
- **No persistent history yet:** Conversations reset when you close the widget (coming in Q1 2026)
- **Basic context:** Helix knows your current page but not your full campaign details yet (coming in Q1 2026)

## For Support Team

### Common User Questions

**Q: "Why can't Helix see my campaign data?"**
A: Enhanced context awareness (seeing campaigns, content, analytics) is coming in Q1 2026. Right now, Helix can only see what page you're on and can query analytics via tool invocations.

**Q: "I closed the chat and lost my conversation. Can I get it back?"**
A: Not yet. Conversation history/persistence is coming in January 2026. For now, conversations reset when you close the widget.

**Q: "Can I upload my brand guidelines so Helix knows my brand?"**
A: This feature (RAG/Knowledge Base) is coming in January 2026. You can manually tell Helix about your brand in each conversation, or use the "update brand DNA" command.

**Q: "Why does Helix keep saying it doesn't know certain things?"**
A: Helix currently has basic context (current page, tier). Full platform integration is rolling out through 2026.

### Upgrade Prompts

**Free users asking about missing features:**
- "This feature is available in the Pro tier. [Upgrade here](/settings)"
- "Document upload will be available to Pro users in January 2026."
