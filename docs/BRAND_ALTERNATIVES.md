# TrendPulse Rebrand Exploration

**Issue:** "TrendPulse" already claimed by competing products. Subdomain launch would get buried in search results.

**Decision Point:** Rebrand before Product Hunt launch (now) vs. launch and rebrand later.

**Recommendation:** Rebrand now. Much cleaner pre-launch.

---

## Core Value Proposition to Capture

- **Problem:** Creators spend 3 hours making content, get 3 likes
- **Solution:** AI analyzes millions of viral posts, shows you the angle that works
- **Benefit:** Create viral-optimized content in minutes, not hours
- **Unique angle:** Trend analysis → content shaping → multi-platform ready

## Top 3 Recommendations (Ranked)

### 🏆 #1: VyralAI
- **Why:** Unique spelling (Vyral not Viral), AI positioning clear, one-word, easy to remember
- **Domain:** vyral.ai or vyralai.io (likely available)
- **Positioning:** "VyralAI - Trend Intelligence for Viral Content"
- **Tagline:** "What if your content was AI-optimized for virality?"

### 🏆 #2: ContentForge
- **Why:** Clear action verb, implies craftsmanship, very SaaS-y
- **Domain:** contentforge.ai or contentforge.io
- **Positioning:** "ContentForge - Forge Viral Moments from Trends"
- **Tagline:** "Crafted for virality, powered by trends"

### 🏆 #3: HookLab
- **Why:** Speaks directly to the core feature (finding the hook/angle), creative positioning
- **Domain:** hooklab.ai or hooklab.io
- **Positioning:** "HookLab - Design Viral Hooks with Trend Intelligence"
- **Tagline:** "The lab where viral hooks are born"

**Rebranding now:**
- ✅ Clean pre-launch
- ✅ Better discoverability
- ✅ Unique positioning
- ⏱️ Costs ~2-4 hours work

**Launching as TrendPulse:**
- ❌ Gets lost in search
- ❌ Confuses users (which TrendPulse?)
- ❌ Harder to rebrand post-launch
- ❌ Product Hunt impact reduced by name confusion
