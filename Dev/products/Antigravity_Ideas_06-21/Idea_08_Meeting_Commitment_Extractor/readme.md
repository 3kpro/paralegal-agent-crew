# PactPull - Meeting Commitment Extractor

> **Status:** Scaffolding Complete 🏗️  
> **Mission:** Extract commitments from meetings and turn them into tracked tasks automatically.

## 🎯 Problem

Sales calls, client meetings, and syncs generate promises that never make it into task trackers. Teams forget what was said, who owns what, and when it's due. The gap between conversation and execution causes missed deliverables.

## 💡 Solution

PactPull transcribes your meeting recordings, uses AI to detect commitments ("I'll send that by Friday"), and exports them as tasks to your project management tool.

- Recording upload and transcription
- AI commitment detection (who, what, when)
- Confidence scoring
- Export to Asana/Linear
- Pending commitments dashboard
