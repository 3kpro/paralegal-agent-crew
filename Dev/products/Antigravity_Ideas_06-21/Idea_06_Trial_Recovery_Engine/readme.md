# TrialRevive - Abandoned Trial Recovery Engine

> **Status:** Scaffolding Complete 🏗️  
> **Mission:** Turn trial abandonment into recovered revenue through AI-powered diagnosis and personalized win-back playbooks.

## 🎯 Problem

SaaS companies convert only 2-5% of free trial signups. The other 95% either never activate, use the product once, or quietly churn. Teams send generic drip emails that get ignored. They lack visibility into *why* specific users didn't convert.

## 💡 Solution

TrialRevive ingests your product analytics, classifies each abandoned trial into actionable segments, and generates personalized recovery strategies — the right message, offer, and timing for each user.
