## Product Description

**Headline:** Turn Trial Abandonment Into Revenue

**Copy:**
95% of your free trials never convert. Most teams blast generic "we miss you" emails and hope for the best. TrialRevive is different. We analyze every abandoned trial's behavior, classify why they left, and generate personalized win-back strategies that actually work.

**Key Features:**
- **Behavioral Forensics:** Know exactly where each user dropped off and why.
- **AI Classification:** Automatically segment churned trials (confused, wrong fit, needs nurturing, competitor shopping).
- **Personalized Playbooks:** Get specific recovery tactics — the right message, offer, and timing for each segment.
- **Integrates With Your Stack:** Pulls from Segment/Mixpanel, pushes to Customer.io/Intercom.
