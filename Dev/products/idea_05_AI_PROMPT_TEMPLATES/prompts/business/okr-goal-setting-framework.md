# OKR Goal Setting Framework
**Category:** Business Operations
**Price:** $(System.Collections.Hashtable.Price)
**Description:** Define clear Objectives and Key Results.

## Full Prompt
Define 3 OKRs for [TEAM/DEPARTMENT] focused on [GOAL]. 
Objective: Qualitative and inspirational.
Key Results: 3-4 Quantitative metrics to measure success.

## Example Output
O: Dominate the US market.
KR: Increase sales by 20%.

## Use Cases
- Management
- Quarterly planning
- Performance tracking
