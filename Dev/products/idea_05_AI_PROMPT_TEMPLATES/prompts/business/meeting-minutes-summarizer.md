# Meeting Minutes Summarizer
**Category:** Business Operations
**Price:** $(System.Collections.Hashtable.Price)
**Description:** Turn raw transcripts into actionable minutes.

## Full Prompt
Summarize this meeting transcript: [TRANSCRIPT]. Output: 
1. Attendees
2. Key Decisions Made
3. Action Items (Who, What, By When)
4. Open Questions.

## Example Output
**Action Items:**
- John to email client by Friday.
- Sarah to fix bug #123.

## Use Cases
- Admin
- Project management
- Record keeping
