# Customer Persona Generator
**Category:** Marketing
**Price:** $(System.Collections.Hashtable.Price)
**Description:** Detailed customer avatars for targeted marketing.

## Full Prompt
Create a detailed customer persona for [PRODUCT/SERVICE]. Include demographics, psychographics, goals, challenges, objections to purchasing, and favorite media channels. Give the persona a name.

## Example Output
Name: Marketing Mary
Age: 32
Role: Marketing Manager
Goals: Increase ROI, Save Time
Pain Points: Too many tools, messy data...

## Use Cases
- Strategy sessions
- Copywriting focus
- Team alignment
