# Competitor Analysis Framework
**Category:** Marketing
**Price:** $(System.Collections.Hashtable.Price)
**Description:** Deep dive analysis of competitor strategies.

## Full Prompt
Perform a SWOT analysis for [COMPETITOR_NAME] based on their publicly available website text: [PASTE_TEXT_OR_URL_DESCRIPTION]. Identify their unique selling proposition, pricing strategy weaknesses, and gaps in their content.

## Example Output
Strength: Strong brand recognition.
Weakness: High price point.
Opportunity: Target their churned users with a migration discount.
