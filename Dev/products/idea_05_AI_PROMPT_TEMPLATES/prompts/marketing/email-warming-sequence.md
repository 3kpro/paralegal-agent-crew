## Full Prompt
Write a 5-day email sequence for new subscribers to [NEWSLETTER_NAME]. The goal is to build trust and establish authority before selling [PRODUCT]. Tone should be specific, helpful, and personal. 

Day 1: Welcome & Value
Day 2: Personal Story/Epiphany
Day 3: Case Study/Social Proof
Day 4: Addressing Objections
Day 5: Soft Pitch
