# Product Launch Email Series
**Category:** Marketing
**Price:** $(System.Collections.Hashtable.Price)
**Description:** A comprehensive 7-email sequence for a major product launch.

## Full Prompt
Create a 7-email product launch sequence for [PRODUCT_NAME]. Features: [KEY_FEATURES]. Benefits: [KEY_BENEFITS]. Launch date: [DATE]. 

Structure:
1. Tease (3 days out)
2. Reveal (Launch Day)
3. Social Proof
4. Logical/Rational Argument
5. Emotional Argument
6. Q&A / FAQs
7. Closing Soon (Last Chance)

## Example Output
Email 1 Subject: Something big is coming...
Email 2 Subject: It's finally here!
...

## Use Cases
- Launcing courses
- SaaS feature release
- New eBook launch
