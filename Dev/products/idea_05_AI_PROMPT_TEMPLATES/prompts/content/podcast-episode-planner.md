# Podcast Episode Planner
**Category:** Content Creation
**Price:** $(System.Collections.Hashtable.Price)
**Description:** Plan interview questions and flow for podcasts.

## Full Prompt
Plan a podcast episode interviewing [GUEST_NAME], expert in [TOPIC]. Goal: Extract actionable advice on [SUBTOPIC]. List 10 questions, starting easy and getting deep. Include 3 'lighting round' fun questions.

## Example Output
1. How did you get started?
2. What was your biggest failure?
...
Lighting Round: Coffee or Tea?

## Use Cases
- Podcast hosts
- YouTube interviews
- Panel moderation
