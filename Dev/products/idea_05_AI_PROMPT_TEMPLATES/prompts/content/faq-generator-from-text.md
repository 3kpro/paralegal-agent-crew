# FAQ Generator from Text
**Category:** Content Creation
**Price:** $(System.Collections.Hashtable.Price)
**Description:** Automatically generate FAQs from product documentation.

## Full Prompt
Read the following product description: [INSERT_TEXT]. Generate 10 Frequently Asked Questions and their answers based ONLY on this text. If the answer isn't there, do not make it up.

## Example Output
Q: How much does it cost?
A: It is /month.

## Use Cases
- Support pages
- Bot training
- Product pages
