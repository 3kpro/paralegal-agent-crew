## Product Description

**Headline:** Never Forget a Commitment Again

**Copy:**
Your team makes dozens of promises every week on calls. "I'll send that over." "Let's circle back Tuesday." "We'll have the draft by EOD." Most of them vanish into the ether. PactPull captures every commitment from your meeting recordings and turns them into trackable tasks.

**Key Features:**
- **AI-Powered Extraction:** Detects who promised what and when.
- **Confidence Scoring:** Know which commitments are clear vs. ambiguous.
- **One-Click Export:** Push directly to Asana, Linear, or Notion.
