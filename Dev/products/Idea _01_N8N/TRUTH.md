# Statement of Truth - n8n Workflow Marketplace

## Core Purpose
Provide pre-built, battle-tested n8n workflows that allow businesses and creators to automate complex tasks instantly without technical expertise.

## Target User
Content creators, small business owners, and solo-preneurs who need automation but lack the time or skill to build complex n8n workflows from scratch.

## Value Proposition
Save 10-50 hours of development time per workflow. Proven reliability vs flaky DIY attempts.
