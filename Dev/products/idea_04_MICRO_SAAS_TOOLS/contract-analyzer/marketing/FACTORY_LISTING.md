## 📝 Full Content

### The Problem
Freelancers and small agencies often sign contracts without professional legal review because lawyers are expensive ($300+/hr). This leads to:
- Delayed payments (bad payment terms)
- Loss of Intellectual Property (bad IP clauses)
- Excessive risk (unlimited liability)

### The Solution
ContractGuard AI serves as your automated first line of defense. Upload any contract (PDF/Word), and our AI engine—trained on common legal pitfalls—scans the document to highlight 20+ critical risks.

### Key Features
- **Instant Risk Analysis:** Uses Claude 3.5 Sonnet to understand complex legal language.
- **Severity Scoring:** Prioritizes issues by Critical, Medium, and Low risk.
- **Plain English:** Explains *why* a clause is dangerous, not just where it is.
- **Exportable Reports:** Generate a professional PDF summary to send to your client or lawyer.
- **Pro Templates:** Access a library of clean, pre-vetted contracts (NDA, SoW, MSA) to start on the right foot.
