## 📌 Headlines
**Name:** ContractGuard AI

## 📝 Description
**Short Description (260 characters):**
Stop signing blind. ContractGuard AI scans your contracts for 20+ hidden risks like net-90 payment terms, broad IP transfers, and unlimited liability. Get a plain-English risk report in seconds. Not legal advice—just smarter signing.

**Long Description:**
Freelancers and agencies lose thousands every year to bad contracts. "Net 90" payment terms, unlimited liability clauses, and accidental IP transfers are buried in legal jargon that most of us skim over.

Legal review costs $300-$1,000+ per contract. ContractGuard AI fixes this. 

**How it works:**
1. **Upload** your contract (PDF, Word, or Text).
2. **Scan** for 20+ common pitfalls using our specialized AI engine.
3. **Get a Report** that explains risks in plain English and suggests fixes.

**Key Features:**
- 🕵️ **Instant Risk Detection**: Flags payment delays, liability traps, and IP issues.
- 📄 **Plain English Reports**: No legalese. Just clear warnings and advice.
- ⚖️ **Template Library**: 10+ professional, pre-vetted legal templates (NDA, SoW, etc).
- 🔒 **Secure**: Your documents are processed privately and never trained on.

**Who is this for?**
Freelancers, consultants, agencies, and small business owners who want to protect themselves without paying a lawyer for every single document.
