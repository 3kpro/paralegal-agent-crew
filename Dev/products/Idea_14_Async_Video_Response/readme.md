# ReplyClip - Async Video Response Platform

> **Status:** Scaffolding Complete 🏗️  
> **Mission:** Make video a conversational medium, not just broadcast.

## 🎯 Problem
Loom is one-way. Zoom requires scheduling. Teams send Loom links via Slack and reply with more Looms, but it's disorganized.

## 💡 Solution
Threaded video conversations with timestamped replies. True async video communication.

## 📊 Core Features (MVP)
- Screen + camera recording
- Video threads (not just links)
- Reply to specific timestamps
- AI transcription and summary
- Simple sharing
