## Vision Statement

Build a profitable portfolio of B2B SaaS products that solve real engineering and compliance problems, launched sequentially with disciplined validation, starting with FairMerge.

---

## Why This Exists

### The Problem
SaaS market rewards focus, not breadth. Launching 21 products simultaneously creates:
- **Diffused Focus:** Can't optimize any single product when attention is split 21 ways
- **Capital Inefficiency:** Infrastructure costs balloon before validating product-market fit
- **Support Overload:** 21 products = 21 support queues, 21 onboarding flows, 21 failure points
- **Brand Confusion:** Unclear positioning when everything is a priority

### Our Answer
- Launch **one product at a time** with full focus
- Validate **product-market fit** before moving to next ($5K MRR validation gate)
- Build **shared infrastructure incrementally** as needs emerge (not speculatively)
- Maintain **product factory model** without factory bloat

### Core Principle
**"One product, full focus, validate, iterate, repeat."**

## Launch Sequence Philosophy

### Principle: Sequential, Not Parallel

**Why FairMerge First (Gemini Enterprise Validation + Velocity Pivot):**
1. **Strategic Pivot (2026-01-28):** Repositioned from "Bias Detector" → "Velocity Engine" (better market fit)
2. **Highest Confidence:** Clear pain (every team wastes time on nitpicks), easy sell ("ship 20% faster" vs. "detect bias")
3. **Data Moat:** Historical review data creates switching costs (sticky retention, compound value)
4. **70% MVP Complete:** Fastest path to revenue (most advanced product in portfolio)
5. **Highest ACV:** $149-$749/month (value-based pricing: saves 5+ eng hours = $500+ monthly value)
6. **"Top 3 Focus":** Gemini Enterprise recommends pausing other 18 products until FairMerge validates with 10 paying customers

**Next Product Selection Criteria (After FairMerge Validates):**
1. **Customer feedback:** What are FairMerge customers asking for? (land-and-expand opportunity)
2. **Infrastructure leverage:** Can we reuse 50%+ of FairMerge tech stack? (auth, billing, analytics)
3. **Market timing:** Is there a window of opportunity closing? (regulatory changes, competitor gaps)
4. **Revenue potential:** Can it realistically hit $5K MRR within 90 days?
5. **Strategic fit:** Does it strengthen portfolio positioning? (same buyer, adjacent problem)

**Current Product #2 Candidates:**
- **BreakingChange** (API Deprecation Watchdog) - 60% complete, engineering buyer overlap
- **SOC2 Autopilot** - High revenue potential ($349-$749/mo), enterprise buyers
- **TrialRevive** (Trial Recovery Engine) - Proven model, fast monetization

## Success Metrics

### Portfolio-Level Targets

**2026 Annual Goals:**
- **Products Launched:** 2-3 products live by year-end
- **Combined MRR:** $15K MRR across portfolio
- **Efficiency:** <3 months from MVP start to first paying customer
- **Capital Efficiency:** Bootstrap all products (no external funding)

**Quarterly Breakdown:**
- **Q1 2026:** $0 MRR (pre-launch) → $1K MRR (FairMerge beta)
- **Q2 2026:** $1K → $5K MRR (FairMerge validated)
- **Q3 2026:** $5K → $10K MRR (FairMerge + Product #2 beta)
- **Q4 2026:** $10K → $15K MRR (FairMerge + Product #2 + Product #3 beta)

### Per-Product Validation Gates

**Gate 1: Launch (MVP Complete)**
- 10 beta customers willing to use product
- Core features working (70% feature completeness acceptable)
- Infrastructure stable (auth, billing, hosting)
- Success: Move to Gate 2 (validation phase)

**Gate 2: Validation ($5K MRR within 90 days)**
- $5K MRR achieved (mix of Team/Growth/Enterprise tiers)
- <15% monthly churn (customers staying)
- Positive problem-solution fit feedback (NPS >30)
- Success: Move to Gate 3 (scale phase) + Select Product #2

**Gate 3: Scale ($10K MRR within 180 days)**
- $10K MRR with positive unit economics (CAC < 3x LTV)
- <10% monthly churn (retention improving)
- Product-market fit confirmed (customers saying "I need this")
- Success: Product is validated, continue scaling + Launch Product #2

**Kill Criteria:**
- If **Gate 2 fails** (< $5K MRR by Month 5): Pivot pricing/positioning OR kill product
- If **consistent negative feedback** on problem-solution fit: Kill product immediately
- If **<$2K MRR by Month 4**: Strong signal to pivot or kill

## What This Is NOT

### We are NOT:
- ❌ Launching all 21 products in parallel (recipe for failure)
- ❌ Building marketplace infrastructure before validating first product (premature optimization)
- ❌ Raising venture capital (bootstrapped by design, profitable from day one)
- ❌ Hiring large team (founder + AI agents + contractors as needed)
- ❌ Chasing vanity metrics (users, signups, page views)

### We ARE:
- ✅ Disciplined about launch sequence (one at a time, validate before next)
- ✅ Obsessed with validation before scaling (revenue, not traction)
- ✅ Building shared infrastructure incrementally (solve real problems, not hypothetical ones)
- ✅ Maintaining focus on revenue over growth (MRR > MAUs)
- ✅ Iterating based on customer feedback (not building in a vacuum)

## Risk Assessment

### High-Risk Scenarios

**Risk 1: FairMerge Fails Validation ($5K MRR)**
- **Likelihood:** Medium (30%)
- **Impact:** High (delays entire roadmap)
- **Mitigation:** Strict kill criteria ($2K MRR by Month 4 triggers pivot decision)
- **Contingency:** Product #2 candidates ready to launch immediately

**Risk 2: Marketplace Infrastructure Premature**
- **Likelihood:** High (common trap for product factories)
- **Impact:** Medium (capital wasted, slows product development)
- **Mitigation:** "Build for 1, then scale" rule enforced in AGENT_CONTRACT.md
- **Monitoring:** Quarterly review of infrastructure ROI

**Risk 3: Product #2 Selection Wrong**
- **Likelihood:** Medium (25%)
- **Impact:** Medium (wastes 3 months of development)
- **Mitigation:** Validate with FairMerge customer feedback first
- **Contingency:** Fast kill if Product #2 fails Gate 2
