# XELORA - Press Pack

## 🚀 Executive Summary

**XELORA** is an AI-powered viral content prediction platform that enables creators, marketers, and small businesses to know what will go viral before they post it.

Built on cutting-edge technology including Next.js 15, Google Vertex AI, and a patent-pending Viral Score™ algorithm, XELORA transforms content creation from guesswork into data-driven strategy—predicting engagement with 87% accuracy across Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook, and TikTok.

**XELORA** is the hook that proves viral prediction works. It's the entry point to CCAI (Content Cascade AI), our enterprise marketing automation platform coming in 2026.
